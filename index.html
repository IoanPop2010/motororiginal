4-Stroke Cycle Engine

750
RPM
Stroke 1
INTAKE
Piston descends. Intake valve opens. Air-fuel mixture drawn in.
Stroke 2
COMPRESSION
Both valves closed. Piston rises. Mixture compressed.
Stroke 3
POWER
Spark ignites mixture. Combustion forces piston down.
Stroke 4
EXHAUST
Exhaust valve opens. Piston rises. Burnt gases expelled.
